# Initial Concept

A literary development platform powered by AI (StoryForge) that uses a squad of specialized agents to assist in creative writing through professional workflows.

# Product Guide: StoryForge

## Vision
StoryForge is a world-class literary development platform that combats AI writing clichés and "vibe coding" through rigorous narrative engineering. Operating under the AI-XP (AI eXtreme Programming) paradigm, it empowers human authors to produce deep, psychologically resonant fiction by orcherstrating a squad of specialized agents.

## Target Users
- **Pro Fiction Writers:** Seeking deep structural, psychological, and consistency aid.
- **AI Enthusiasts:** Exploring the frontiers of high-quality AI-driven storytelling.
- **Worldbuilders:** Needing robust tools for lore consistency and complex setting development.
- **Story Starters:** Anyone looking for a professional-grade starting point for a new narrative history.

## Core Objectives
- **Abolish Clichés:** Use CHI methods and negative guardrails to prevent common AI tropes and purple prose.
- **Consistency Protection:** Maintain narrative integrity via a systemic Story Bible powered by RAG.
- **Privacy First:** Absolute offline-first architecture; your stories stay on your machine.
- **User-Centric Simplicity:** Professional power delivered through an easy and simple interface.

## MVP Features
### Phase 1 & 2: Structural Planning
- **CHI Ideation:** Anti-cliché worldbuilding and premise generation.
- **Psychological Profiles:** OCEAN/Hauge-based character construction.
- **Narrative Architect:** Multi-framework support (Save the Cat, Hero's Journey, etc.).

### Phase 3: Guided Writing (E.P.R.L.)
- **Prosa Assistant:** Gearing production of 500-800 words per beat with strict stylistic guardrails.
- **Story Bible Integration:** Context-aware prose generation using RAG.

### Phase 5: Structural Auditing
- **Bad Cop Editor:** Heuristic detection of AI-isms, syntax repetition, and adverb density.
- **Causal Analysis:** Ensuring every scene is connected by logic, not coincidence.

## Technical Foundation
- **Platform:** Desktop application built with Rust (Tauri) for system safety and speed.
- **Interface:** distraction-free UI using React + Tailwind CSS.
- **Persistence:** Local SQLite with vector extensions for semantic search.
