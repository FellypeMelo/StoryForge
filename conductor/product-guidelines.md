# Product Guidelines: StoryForge

## Prose & Content Style
- **Evocative Engineering:** Prose must be rich and emotionally resonant but strictly controlled.
- **Anti-Cliché Guardrails:** Actively block common AI tropes (e.g., "shimmering", "tapestry of emotions").
- **Precision over Fluff:** Every sentence must serve a purpose—either characterization, plot advancement, or thematic depth.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Distraction-Free Writing:** The interface should vanish when the author is in the flow state.
- **Power with Simplicity:** Complex engineering tools (RAG, structural auditing) must be intuitive and accessible.
- **Instant Reactive Feedback:** Provide real-time signals on narrative health without interrupting the creative process.

## Visual Identity & Branding
- **Dark & Typographic Aesthetic:** Deep tones and high contrast to reduce eye strain, with a primary focus on elegant, readable typography.
- **Minimalist Layout:** Eliminate unnecessary UI elements; prioritize the manuscript and active agent feedback.

## Narrative Engineering Process
- **Psychology-First:** Characters must be built from the "Wound" outward to ensure authentic agency and growth.
- **Logical Causality Enforcement:** Every scene connection must follow the "Therefore / But" rule to maintain narrative tension.
- **Iterative Audit Cycle:** The "Bad Cop" editor runs continuously in the background, signaling structural weaknesses and AI-isms for immediate human refinement.
- **Human-Centric 70/30 Rule:** AI provides the 30% structural tração; humans provide the 70% unique soul and fine modeling.
